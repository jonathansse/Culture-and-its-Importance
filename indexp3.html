    Culture with a modern perspective

    Putting into consideration the digital age and how the world is developing, most people are adapting and joining the Western Culture instead of preserving their own. The world culture no longer has a strong meaning in this world and that needs to be changed. The youth population are the main people encouraging this change and adaptation to the Western Culture. Culture has grown as a part of society and affected the different perceptions generated by society.

    Importance

    Culture is a very important part of our identity because culture is what leads our lives. To define culture, culture is a way of life. The way one lives is a very important part of their identity and therefore culture is important as a part of our identity. It is important to know how important culture is as a part of our identity because it consumes a large section of our identity and it is what determines how we react to different things, our abilities and other capabilities we have.

    Societal diversity is also an important part of culture. Societal diversity is extremely important because since most communities seek development. By having a wide range of diversity and different people. These people encourage new ideas and new lifestyles which typically are incorporated for the benefit of the community.

    Cultural diversity and combination augments the introduction to new food, art, design, clothing, infrastructure and it opens new opportunities to business. It also encourages culture integration. This also gives people the chance to experience new things, and bring in new skills, attitudes and perceptions.

    Influence

    Our culture refers to our shared values, beliefs and norms. Culture hence influences the way we live, learn and behave and due to this culture shapes the way we live in a very effective manner. Culture plays a big role upon our beliefs, values and norms and greatly influences the way in which we live, learn, behave and react towards different situations.
